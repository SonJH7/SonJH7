### 👋 About Me

I'm an **undergraduate researcher** at the **Quantum Artificial Intelligence Lab (Quailab)**, School of Computer Science and Engineering, **Pusan National University**, advised by **Prof. Won-Joo Hwang**.

My research is about making **dense, constraint-heavy QUBO workloads run *reproducibly* on real quantum annealers (D-Wave Advantage2)**, applied to **molecular docking** in structure-based drug discovery. I also build web applications, with a frontend background from the **Kakao Tech Campus** program.

- 🔬 **Quantum annealing** · QUBO formulation · hardware-aware tuning (penalty scaling, minor-embedding, chain strength, anneal schedules)
- 🧬 **Molecular docking** · feature-atom-matching (FAM) QUBO encoding · Bayesian optimization
- 💻 **Frontend engineering** (React / JavaScript)

### 🚀 Featured Research

> **QDock: Hardware-Aware Quantum Annealing for Molecular Docking**
> First-author work, presented as a poster at **AQC 2026** (Adiabatic Quantum Computing Conference, Tokyo).
> A calibration framework that runs **Bayesian optimization over a physically-bounded penalty domain**, then sweeps **chain strength / anneal time / schedule shape** under a *frozen, embedding-isolated* configuration, producing **target-specific, reproducible recipes** for docking QUBOs on sparse-connectivity QPUs.
